FAQ:

Dear customer - Here you will find help on the following topics:
    + How do I navigate the site? +

    There are two ways to navigate our site. The first one is the blue navigation bar at the top. The second are the buttons embedded on the pages. The navigation bar features on the left-hand side the buttons "Home", which takes you back to the homepage and "Help", which takes you here. On the right side it will feature "Login" and "Register" if you are not logged in yet. Use these buttons to log in and register respectively.

    If you are already logged in, the buttons "Book seat" and "Logout" are displayed instead. Use "Book seat" to be guided through the seat registration process. If you select "Logout", you will be safely logged out and returned to the homepage after a short time.

    + How do I access my account? +

    To use the system, you must have an account.

    You can create an account by clicking on "Register". The registration page will guide you through the registration process. If you already have an account, you can log in to your account by clicking on "Login". If you are not logged in yet, you will find both buttons at the top right of the navigation bar.

    + How do I register? +

    To register click on "Register". The page that opens will guide you through the registration process.

    + How do I reserve a seat? +

    To reserve a seat, you first need an account, and you must be logged in (see: How do I access my account?).

    After you have logged in, you will automatically be taken to the booking page. Here you first select the flight for which you want a seat. Then you can choose one of the seats that have not yet been allocated. The first row represents the headers for the columns that represent seats; the first column represents the seat rows in the airplane. The seats on the outside are window seats, the vertical lines represent the aisle. Available seats are shown in GREEN . A Seat already reserved is represented by a RED X.
